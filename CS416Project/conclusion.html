Carbon Dioxide Global Trends

Concluding Thoughts

Through this visualization we were able to see the change in global CO2 emissions over the last several decades. Although we did not see any notable outliers when comparing GDP to CO2 output per country, we did note that there has been an unmistakable increase in overall CO2 emissions on both a national and international level.

This visualization was created for the Summer 2021 term of CS 416 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. The graphs were generated using the co2-data published by Our World in Data under the Creative Commons License.
