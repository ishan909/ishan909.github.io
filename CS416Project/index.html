Carbon Dioxide Global Trends

About this visualization

Carbon Dioxide (CO2) is argued to be one of the primary contributor to climate change. Many claim that we need a significant reduction in CO2 emissions to reduce the impact that we leave on the planet.

In this visualization, we will investigate how CO2 emissions have changed over the past 150+ years. We will also examine which regions of the world contribute most to this problem.
